## Batik Air

![Batik Air][25]![Batik Air][26]

Batik Air is a domestic and international flight provider operating in Indonesia. We have developed a user-friendly mobile application for their flight booking services, offering customers the convenience of booking seats, baggage, meals, and medical kits if needed. Passengers can also complete online check-in to avoid crowds at the check-in counter.

## Gmat

![Gmat][25]![Gmat][26]

A one-stop solution for all your insurance needs in Malaysia. We have developed a single application that aggregates all available insurance options in Malaysia, including Motor Insurance, Travel Insurance, Insurance Rahmah, Maid and hikers Insurance more. This application simplifies the process for customers by allowing them to purchase all their insurance requirements in a single website visit, ensuring the most cost-effective and optimal insurance choices.

## Getherly

![Getherly][25]![Getherly][26]

Getherly is a new-era social media application that offers various features found in major social media platforms. Our application includes a chat option, a group chat option, an audio call option, a video call option, a group audio call or audio conference, and a group video call or group conference. In addition, Getherly allows users to post updates, view posts, create and view stories, and has a special feature known as the family tree concept.
