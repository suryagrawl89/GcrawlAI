## FAQ - Gen AI Services by GramoSoft

![faq][59]

## What is Generative AI and how can GramoSoft help?

Generative AI uses advanced models like GPT to create intelligent content and automate workflows. GramoSoft helps you implement Gen AI to streamline operations and boost productivity.

## What Gen AI services does GramoSoft offer?

We provide end-to-end services including AI agent development, LLM testing & fine-tuning, custom Gen AI model development, model replication, GPT integration, ML integration and AI consulting.

## Can GramoSoft integrate ChatGPT or GPT-4 into our platform?

Yes! GramoSoft specializes in integrating GPT-3.5, GPT-4 and GPT-4o into your apps, websites, CRM tools or internal systems.

## Does GramoSoft offer domain specific fine-tuning for LLMs?

Absolutely. We fine-tune LLMs using your business or industry data, ensuring accurate, safe and context aware results.

## Which industries does GramoSoft serve with Gen AI?

GramoSoft supports clients across startups, SMEs, NGOs and corporates in sectors like healthcare, finance, education, logistics and more.

## How long does it take GramoSoft to deploy a Gen AI solution?

Typical projects take 2 to 6 weeks depending on complexity, data availability and integration requirements.

## Does GramoSoft provide Gen AI consulting before starting development?

Yes. We begin with a strategic consultation to identify the right Gen AI use cases, tools and architecture for your business.

## Is our data secure with GramoSoft during development?

100%. GramoSoft follows enterprise grade data security standards and offers deployment through secure APIs, private cloud or on-premise systems.
