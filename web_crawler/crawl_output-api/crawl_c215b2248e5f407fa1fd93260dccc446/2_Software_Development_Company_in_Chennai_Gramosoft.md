#### Privacy Policy for Gramosoft

At Gramosoft, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and protect your personal information in connection with our website, services, products and related materials (collectively, the "Services"). By using our Services, you consent to the collection, use, disclosure and protection of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We're glad to have you on board and appreciate your support of our terms as you use our Services!

#### Collection and Use of Personal Information

We may collect personal information from you in connection with your use of our Services, including:

  * Contact Information: Name, email address, mailing address and phone number.
  * Usage Information: IP address, browser type, operating system and pages visited.
  * Communications: Messages you send to us or information provided in surveys or promotions.
  * Other Information: Any additional information you choose to provide.


We use the personal information we collect for various purposes, such as providing our Services to you, communicating about our Services, personalizing your experience on our website, conducting research and analysis to improve our Services, complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights.

#### Disclosure of Personal Information

We may disclose your personal information to service providers, affiliates, business partners and when necessary to comply with legal requirements or protect our rights. We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.

#### Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites that are not owned or controlled by us. We encourage you to review their privacy policies and terms of use as we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites.

#### Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information and disclaim any liability for unauthorized access.

#### How We Process Personal Data

Personal data is processed in accordance with the GDPR. The personal data collected by Gramosoft will be processed lawfully and fairly, collected for specified lawful purposes, accurate and adequate and stored no longer than necessary.

#### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in response to legal, technical, or business developments. Significant changes will be communicated through our Services or other means.

#### General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. Your rights include the right of access, right to rectification, right to restriction, right to object, right to erasure, right to data portability and right to withdraw consent.

#### India Consumer Privacy Act (ICPA)

If you are an Indian resident, you have the right to request disclosure of certain information about our collection, use and disclosure of your personal information over the past 12 months. You also have the right to request deletion of your personal information.

#### Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.
