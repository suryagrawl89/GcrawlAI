#### About Numentica Consulting Group

###### Comprehensive Service Portfolio:

  * Our service portfolio encompasses a wide range of capabilities designed to support customers across various sectors and business functions

###### Data-Driven Innovation:

  * A foundation of well-governed, timely, and trusted data is essential for activating cutting-edge use cases like Generative AI and RPA

###### Strategic Partnerships:

  * We partner with technology leaders such as Microsoft, Databricks, Salesforce, and Informatica empowering our team of 500+ engineers and consultants to craft next-generation enterprise solutions.

###### Product Engineering Excellence:

  * We specialize in both backend and frontend services, delivering top-notch product engineering solutions for our customers and their stakeholders.

###### Future-Focused Solutions:

  * Through our startup and staffing solutions, we assist future unicorns and entrepreneurs in developing innovative solutions that will impact the lives of the next generation.
