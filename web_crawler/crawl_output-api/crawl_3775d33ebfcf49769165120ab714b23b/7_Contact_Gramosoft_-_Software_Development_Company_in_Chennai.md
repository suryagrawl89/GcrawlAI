## FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

![faq][49]

## How does Gramo Contact help entrepreneurs?

Gramo Contact helps entrepreneurs by making networking easier and more effective. Features like photo tagging, location memory and QR sharing allow entrepreneurs to remember contacts, follow up quickly and build meaningful business relationships after events, conferences, or meetings.

## Is my data secure and backed up?

We provide Yes! Gramo Contact uses real-time Firebase sync with enterprise-grade encryption to keep your network safe.

## Can I import my existing contacts?

Absolutely. Import/export via CSV and our smart merge cleans duplicates automatically.

## How does QR sharing work?

Generate a QR code with your full contact profile. Others can scan it instantly, no manual typing required.

## What makes Gramo Contact different from built-in apps?

Unlike standard apps, Gramo Contact offers photo tagging, GPS memory, smart duplicate merging, QR code exchange and unlimited group management - all designed for entrepreneurs.
